OPTIMAL SUBARRAY X-FACTOR

Given an array of integers nums and an integer k, find the maximum "X-Factor" of any subarray of length at least k.

The X-Factor is defined as the product of the bitwise XOR sum of the subarray and its length.

Example 1:

Input: nums = [1, 2, 3], k = 2

Output: 6

Explanation: Subarray [2, 3] has XOR sum (2 ^ 3) = 1. Length is 2. Product = 2. Subarray [1, 2, 3] has XOR sum (1 ^ 2 ^ 3) = 0. Length is 3. Product = 0. Max is 6.

CONSTRAINTS:

  • 1 ≤ nums.length ≤ 10^5
  • 0 ≤ nums[i] ≤ 10^9
  • 1 ≤ k ≤ nums.length
